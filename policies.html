Store Policies

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Any personal information (name, address, photos) shared with us over WhatsApp or our website is strictly used for fulfilling your customized order and is never shared with third parties.

Customization Policy

Since our products are personalized specifically for you, we cannot accept cancellations once production has started. Please ensure all names, messages, and uploaded photos are accurate before submitting your order.

Shipping Policy

Orders are dispatched within 2-4 business days after payment confirmation. Delivery times vary by location but typically take 3-5 days across India.

Return & Refund Policy

Due to the customized nature of our gifts, we do not accept returns. However, if your item arrives damaged or there is an error on our part, please contact us on WhatsApp with an unboxing video within 24 hours of delivery, and we will arrange a replacement.
