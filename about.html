About Us

Our Story

Sk Studio Gift is a premium customized gift shop based in Nidagundi, Karnataka. We started with a simple belief: that every gift should tell a story, capture a precious moment, and hold a special memory.

Over time, we have grown into a trusted local destination offering a wide range of personalized items. From high-quality photo frames and glowing LED frames to romantic couple gifts, birthday surprises, elegant wedding gifts, and thoughtful return gifts.

Our goal is to provide top-notch craftsmanship with an emotional touch. We pride ourselves on creating a customer-friendly experience, ensuring that your customized gifts are not just products, but timeless memories made with love.
