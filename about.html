Our Story

Sk Studio Gift is a premium customized gift shop based in Nidagundi, Karnataka. We believe that every gift should tell a story and hold a special memory.

We offer a wide range of personalized items including high-quality photo frames, glowing LED frames, romantic couple gifts, birthday and anniversary surprises, personalized mugs, crystal gifts, keychains, and elegant wedding gifts.

Our goal is to provide top-notch craftsmanship with an emotional touch, ensuring that your customized gifts are not just products, but timeless memories made with love.
