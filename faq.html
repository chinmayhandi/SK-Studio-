Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Customization
How do I place an order for a customized gift?
To place an order, select your desired product, choose customization options (like size), and click "Proceed to Checkout". Enter your delivery details and click "Confirm & Order via WhatsApp". Our system will automatically format your order details and send them to us on WhatsApp.
How do I send photos for my customized order?
Please send the required number of photos in Document Format directly on WhatsApp after confirming your order. Note: Once photos are sent and production begins, photos cannot be changed.
Can I preview my customized gift before printing?
Yes! After you send your photos on WhatsApp, our admin will create a design and send you a digital preview. Once you approve the preview, your gift will go into final printing and production.
Shipping & Delivery
What are your shipping options and delivery times?
We offer two options: "Store Collect" (Free) and "Home Delivery". Home delivery generally takes 5-7 business working days after the product has been dispatched.
How much does Home Delivery cost?
Delivery charges are calculated based on your pincode. The exact delivery charge will be communicated to you by our admin while you are paying for your order on WhatsApp.
Product & Quality
What is the quality of your printed products?
We use premium materials and state-of-the-art printing technology to ensure the highest quality. Our frames are handcrafted and designed to preserve your memories for a lifetime.
Payment & Pricing
Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?
No, we currently do not offer Cash on Delivery. All orders are customized and must be paid for in advance before production begins.
How do I make a payment?
Once your order details are confirmed on WhatsApp, the admin will send you a secure payment link or QR code (UPI/Bank Transfer).
Are there any hidden costs?
No hidden costs! The base price of the product is clearly shown on the website. If you choose home delivery, the exact shipping cost will be clearly communicated to you on WhatsApp before you make any payment. If there are any discount offers applied, they will be calculated on the full product price (delivery charges are not discounted).
