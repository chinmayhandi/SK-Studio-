Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send photos for my customized order?
You can upload the required photos directly on the product order page. After submitting the details, you will be redirected to WhatsApp. Please attach and send the same photos in the WhatsApp chat to confirm your order.
Do you offer cash on delivery (COD)?
Currently, all orders are processed via WhatsApp where we will provide you with payment details (UPI/Bank Transfer). Once payment is confirmed, your customized order goes into production.
How long does delivery take?
Customized gifts usually take 2-4 days for production. Shipping time depends on your location, generally taking another 3-5 business days.
